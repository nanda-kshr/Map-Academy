SUPRAJA POTHUKUCHI



Born in Tirumala, India, she moved to Seattle in 2002 after completing her bachelor's degree and getting married. She has two children and found her passion for working with kids while volunteering at her child's school. After a brief stint as a software tester, she began working in the Lake Washington School District. She now works as a paraeducator and aspires to start a nonprofit for children with disabilities and open a preschool and daycare in the future.

Her Vision



At her center, she ensures that children engage in daily challenging activities, emphasizing the importance of a friendly and clean learning environment that supports their development. As a teacher, she models learning for children, recognizing the crucial role of play in their daily lives for building social skills. Her structured curriculum aids in managing their schedules and learning, with meaningful teacher-child interactions. She also values connecting with families to support children's growth, holding regular meetings and providing feedback. Her inspiration to become a teacher came from observing children in her friend circle, learning from their determination and quest for success.
